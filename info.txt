Some Information about the Input Features:

1) Year - The year in which the car was purchased
2) Age - Age of the car in years
3) Distance Driven - The overall distance travelled by the car in kilometers
4) Fuel - Type of fuel used by the car (Petrol, Diesel, CNG)
5) Seller Type - Type of Seller (Individual, Dealer)
6) Transmission Type - Type of transmission of the car (Manual, Automatic)
7) Owner - Number of previous owner of the car
8) Present Price - Current showroom price of the car
